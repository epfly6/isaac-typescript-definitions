Enumeration BossID

For StageID.SPECIAL_ROOMS (0), RoomType.ROOM_BOSS (5)

This matches the sub-type in the "00.special rooms.stb" file. The enum is named this way to match the Entity.GetBossID, Room.GetBossID and Room.GetSecondBossID methods.
